Turkey Sub

Description

This has always been my favorite sandwich. Just bread, turkey and mayo.

Ingredients

  • hoagie bun
  • Turkey
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Cut your hoagie bun in half, make sure not to cut yourself
  2. Lightly toast hoagie bun until bread is crisp
  3. Place turkey on one half
  4. Add desired amount of mayo on the other half
