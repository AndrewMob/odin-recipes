Country Club

>

Description

This sandwich has it's origins in Country clubs all across america but how I first learn how to make it was at loafin' joe's. The sandwich is topped with american, mozzarella, and bacon. Turkey, lettuce, tomato and mayo complete the sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Turkey
  • Bacon
  • American cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayo

Steps : Making the sandwich

  1. Depending on the choice of bread you should either cut the bread in half or get 2 slices of cut bread
  2. Place the turkey on the bottom of the sandwich
  3. Place the American cheese on the top of the sandwich first
  4. Cover the American cheese with the mozzarella cheese to prevent the american cheese from burning
  5. Place precooked Bacon on top of the cheeses
  6. Cook @ 400 degrees F for 7 minutes with both slices open
  7. Add your preferred amount of Mayo, lettuce and tomato
  8. Enjoy your Country Club!
